# **App Name**: eHut

## Core Features:

- Product Showcase: Detailed product pages featuring high-quality images and videos of natural products like mangoes, dates, molasses, and honey.
- Social Sharing: Seamless social media integration with one-click sharing to Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok, allowing users to easily share their favorite products and promotions.
- AI-Powered Share Tool: AI analyses your social network's interests to customize and target product sharing to increase user engagement.
- Shopping Cart and Checkout: Intuitive and secure shopping cart and checkout process optimized for the Bangladeshi market, including local payment gateway integrations.
- Brand Story and Traceability: Dedicated section showcasing the brand's story, origin, and commitment to natural products, providing full traceability information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Golden yellow (#FFC107) to evoke warmth and natural energy.
- Background color: Desaturated terracotta (#E0A96F) for an earthy, grounding feel.
- Accent color: Rich green (#4CAF50) to represent the natural origin and freshness of the products.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif for elegance, readability and a slightly formal feel.
- Use natural, organic-shaped icons to represent the products and their origins.
- Clean, grid-based layout with plenty of white space to highlight the product images and descriptions.
- Subtle animations, such as image transitions and loading spinners, to enhance the user experience without being distracting.
